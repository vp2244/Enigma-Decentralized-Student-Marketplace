Welcome to Enigma Marketplace

Buy and sell student goods with EnigCredit (ENGC) — trustless, on-chain.

💳
Token & Wallet
Connect your wallet and check your ENGC balance. Faculty can mint demo credits.
🏷️
Browse Listings
Post items for sale and browse what's available — textbooks, electronics, notes, and more.
🤝
Escrow / Trade
Purchase items securely. Tokens held in escrow until delivery is confirmed.
⭐
Reputation
Rate sellers after a completed trade. On-chain reputation builds community trust.
⚡ Getting Started
ℹ️ Deploy contracts and paste addresses into shared/config.js — then connect your wallet to begin.
1. Go to Token & Wallet — connect MetaMask and verify your ENGC balance.
2. Go to Browse Listings — post an item or click Buy Now on an available listing.
3. Complete the trade in Escrow / Trade — approve ENGC, confirm delivery.
4. Leave a rating in Reputation to help future buyers.
