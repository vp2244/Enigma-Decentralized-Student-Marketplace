Enigma comprises three Solidity 0.8.20 contracts built on OpenZeppelin v5.1.0 --- \texttt{EnigCredit} (ERC-20), \texttt{Marketplace} (listings and escrow), and \texttt{Reputation} --- plus a vanilla JavaScript/ethers.js front end that targets either a local Anvil chain (chainId 31337) or Sepolia (11155111) selected at runtime. Reads use a JSON-RPC provider; state-changing calls are signed through the user's wallet.

The threat model assumes mutually distrusting buyers and sellers and a hostile network observer. Key risks and mitigations are: unauthorized credit issuance, prevented by an \texttt{Ownable} owner-only \texttt{mint}; payment theft or double-spend during settlement, prevented by holding funds in escrow and applying the checks-effects-interactions pattern with \texttt{ReentrancyGuard} and \texttt{SafeERC20}; and reputation forgery, prevented by binding each rating to a verified, completed sale. \texttt{Pausable} provides an emergency stop.
