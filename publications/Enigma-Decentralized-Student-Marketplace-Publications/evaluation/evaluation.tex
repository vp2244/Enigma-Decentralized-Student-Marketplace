We evaluate Enigma with the Foundry toolchain~\cite{foundry}. The token slice's unit suite \texttt{EnigCreditTest} passes 7 of 7 cases, covering metadata, initial supply, owner mint, non-owner-mint revert, transfer, permit, and burn. The contracts were deployed both to a local Anvil chain and to the public Sepolia testnet (EnigCredit at \texttt{0x3162{\ldots}F0de5}); through the hosted web client the owner successfully minted ENGC and non-owner mints were correctly rejected with \texttt{CALL\_EXCEPTION} on both networks, confirming the access-control property end-to-end. Table~\ref{tab:status} summarizes per-slice status; the listings, escrow, and reputation suites are reported by their owners as those slices are completed.
