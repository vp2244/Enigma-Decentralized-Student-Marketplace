Enigma is a decentralized peer-to-peer marketplace for students, realized as four cooperating Ethereum smart contracts and a single dual-network web client. Participants transact in **EnigCredit (ENGC)**, an ERC-20 credit token; listings are recorded on-chain; payments sit in **escrow** until delivery is confirmed; completed trades produce tamper-evident **reputation**. We describe the modular *vertical-slice* architecture, the security properties of each contract, and an evaluation on local Anvil and public Sepolia.
